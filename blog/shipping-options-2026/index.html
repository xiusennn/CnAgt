Shipping Options from China 2026: Complete Guide

Shipping from China has evolved significantly in 2026, with more options than ever before. Whether you need your package delivered tomorrow or you're willing to wait a month for the best price, there's a shipping method that fits your needs.

Express Shipping (DHL, FedEx, UPS)

Express shipping is the fastest option, typically delivering within 5-10 business days to most countries. Major carriers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS offer door-to-door service with full tracking and insurance options.

Best for: Urgent orders, high-value items, and time-sensitive purchases.

Cost: Starting from $25/kg, with minimum charges of $30-40 per shipment.

Standard Air Mail

Standard air mail through China Post or ePacket offers a good balance between cost and speed. Delivery typically takes 15-30 business days depending on the destination country.

Best for: Regular orders where speed isn't critical but you still want reasonable delivery times.

Cost: Starting from $12/kg, making it significantly cheaper than express options.

Sea Freight

Sea freight is the most economical option for large or heavy shipments. While it takes 30-60 days, the cost per kilogram is dramatically lower than air shipping methods.

Best for: Bulk orders, heavy items, and non-urgent shipments.

Cost: Starting from $5/kg, ideal for orders over 20kg.
