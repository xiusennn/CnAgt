How to Buy from 1688.com: Complete Guide 2026

Learn how to buy from 1688.com as a foreign buyer. Complete guide covering payment methods, shipping options, and how to use a purchasing agent.

What is 1688.com?

1688.com is China's largest wholesale marketplace, owned by Alibaba Group. It connects buyers directly with factories and wholesalers across China, offering products at significantly lower prices than retail platforms like Taobao or international sites like AliExpress. The platform hosts over 10 million suppliers and 100 million products across every category imaginable.

For foreign buyers, 1688.com represents an incredible opportunity to source products at factory-direct prices. However, the platform is entirely in Chinese, requires a Chinese bank account or Alipay for payment, and most sellers don't ship internationally. This is where a purchasing agent becomes essential.

Key Advantage: Products on 1688.com are typically 30-70% cheaper than the same items on AliExpress or Amazon. For bulk orders, the savings can be even more substantial.

Why Buy from 1688.com?

There are several compelling reasons to source from 1688.com rather than international platforms:

  • Factory-Direct Prices: Most sellers on 1688 are manufacturers or authorized distributors, cutting out middlemen and offering wholesale pricing.
  • Massive Product Selection: From electronics and clothing to industrial equipment and home goods, 1688 has virtually everything made in China.
  • Custom Manufacturing: Many 1688 suppliers offer OEM/ODM services, allowing you to customize products with your branding.
  • Low Minimum Order Quantities: Unlike traditional wholesale, many 1688 sellers accept orders as small as 1-2 pieces.
  • Quality Verification: Suppliers with "Power Merchant" or "Super Factory" badges have been verified by Alibaba.

How to Navigate 1688.com as a Foreign Buyer

Since 1688.com is entirely in Chinese, here's how to navigate the platform effectively:

1. Use Browser Translation

The easiest way to browse 1688 is using Google Chrome's built-in translation feature. Right-click anywhere on the page and select "Translate to English." While not perfect, it gives you a good understanding of product listings, prices, and seller information.

2. Search by Image

1688.com has a powerful image search feature. If you've seen a product on Amazon or another site, save the image and upload it to 1688's search bar. You'll often find the exact same product at a fraction of the price.

3. Understand the Pricing

1688 prices are shown in Chinese Yuan (CNY). Most products have tiered pricing — the more you buy, the lower the unit price. Always check the MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity) before placing an order.

4. Check Seller Credentials

Look for these indicators of reliable sellers:

  • 牛头 (Bull Head): Indicates a "Power Merchant" — verified and trusted seller.
  • Super Factory: Verified manufacturing facility.
  • Years in Business: Sellers with 3+ years on the platform are generally more reliable.
  • Transaction Volume: Higher sales numbers indicate popularity and trust.

Payment Methods on 1688.com

Payment is the biggest challenge for foreign buyers on 1688.com. The platform primarily accepts:

  1. Alipay: The most common payment method. Requires a Chinese bank account or international credit card (with limitations).
  2. Bank Transfer: Direct transfer to the seller's Chinese bank account.
  3. WeChat Pay: Some sellers accept WeChat Pay, but still requires Chinese payment setup.

Shipping from 1688.com to Your Country

Most 1688 sellers only ship within China. Here's how to get your products internationally:

Option 1: Use a Purchasing Agent (Recommended)

A purchasing agent in China receives your 1688 orders at their warehouse, inspects the products, consolidates multiple orders into one package, and ships them to your international address. This is the most convenient and reliable method.

Option 2: Use a Freight Forwarder

Freight forwarders provide a Chinese address for sellers to ship to, then forward your packages internationally. However, they typically don't offer quality inspection or purchasing assistance.

Shipping Costs and Times

Shipping costs vary by weight, dimensions, and destination:

  • Express (DHL/FedEx): 3-7 days, $25-50/kg
  • Air Freight: 7-15 days, $15-25/kg
  • Sea Freight: 30-45 days, $5-10/kg (best for heavy items)

Common Mistakes to Avoid on 1688.com

  1. Not Checking Reviews: Always read buyer reviews and check the seller's transaction history.
  2. Ignoring MOQ: Some products require minimum orders of 100+ pieces. Always verify before ordering.
  3. Assuming Photos Are Accurate: Product photos can be misleading. Request actual photos from the seller before purchasing.
  4. Not Accounting for Shipping: Factor in shipping costs when comparing prices. A cheap product with expensive shipping may not be a good deal.
  5. Skipping Quality Inspection: Always have your agent inspect products before international shipping. Returns to China are expensive and complicated.
