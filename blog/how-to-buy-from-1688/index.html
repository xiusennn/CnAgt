How to Buy from 1688.com: Complete Guide 2026

Master 1688.com shopping as an international buyer. Learn supplier selection, factory ratings, shipping costs, and how to use a purchasing agent.

What Is 1688.com?

1688.com is China's largest wholesale marketplace, owned by Alibaba Group. Unlike Taobao (which targets individual consumers), 1688 connects buyers directly with factories, wholesalers, and distributors. This means significantly lower prices — often 30-50% cheaper than retail platforms — but with minimum order quantities (MOQ) and a more complex purchasing process.

For international buyers, 1688 presents both enormous opportunity and significant challenges. The platform is entirely in Chinese, requires domestic payment methods (Alipay China, WeChat Pay, Chinese bank cards), and most suppliers don't offer international shipping. That's where a purchasing agent becomes essential.

Key Insight: 1688.com is the same company behind Alibaba.com, but focused on the domestic Chinese market. Prices on 1688 are typically 20-40% lower than on Alibaba.com because you're buying directly from factories without export markup.

How to Choose the Right Supplier on 1688

Finding a reliable supplier is the most critical step in buying from 1688. Here's a systematic approach to evaluating potential suppliers:

1. Check the Supplier Badge System

2. Evaluate Key Metrics

Beyond badges, check these specific metrics on every supplier's profile page:

  • Years in Business (经营年限): Look for suppliers with 3+ years on 1688. Longer tenure generally indicates stability and reliability.
  • Repeat Purchase Rate (回头率): This shows the percentage of buyers who return for additional orders. A rate above 20% is good; above 30% is excellent.
  • Transaction Volume (成交额): Higher transaction volumes indicate active, trusted suppliers. Check the monthly trend — consistent or growing is ideal.
  • Response Rate (响应率): Suppliers who respond quickly to inquiries are more likely to provide good after-sales service.
  • Dispute Rate (纠纷率): Lower is better. A dispute rate below 5% indicates reliable products and service.

3. Verify Product Quality

Before placing a large order, always request samples. Look at product photos carefully — suppliers with professional, detailed photos tend to be more legitimate. Check customer reviews and look for real photos uploaded by previous buyers, not just stock images.

Understanding 1688 Shipping & Cost Calculation

Shipping from 1688 involves multiple cost layers. Understanding these helps you budget accurately and avoid surprises:

Domestic Shipping (Within China)

Most 1688 suppliers offer free or low-cost domestic shipping within China. Typical costs:

  • Free shipping: Many suppliers offer free shipping for orders above a certain amount (usually ¥30-100).
  • Standard domestic shipping: ¥8-15 per package for smaller orders.
  • Express domestic shipping: ¥15-25 for SF Express (顺丰) or other premium carriers.

When using a purchasing agent, products are shipped to the agent's warehouse in China first. This domestic shipping is usually included in the agent's service fee.

International Shipping Cost Calculation

International shipping from China is calculated based on actual weight or volumetric weight, whichever is greater:

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost (per kg) Best For
Express (DHL/FedEx/UPS) 3-7 days $25-50 Urgent, high-value items
Air Freight 7-15 days $15-25 Medium-weight packages (2-10kg)
Sea Freight 30-45 days $5-10 Heavy, bulky items (10kg+)
Rail Freight (Europe) 18-25 days $10-18 European destinations

Volumetric Weight Formula: Length (cm) × Width (cm) × Height (cm) ÷ 5000 = Volumetric Weight (kg). If this exceeds actual weight, you'll be charged based on volumetric weight. Your purchasing agent can help consolidate packages to minimize volumetric weight.

Additional Costs to Consider

  • Customs duties: Varies by destination country. Most countries have a de minimis value below which no duty is charged.
  • Insurance: Recommended for high-value shipments, typically 1-3% of declared value.
  • Packaging materials: Bubble wrap, cartons, and protective packaging add ¥5-20 per package.
  • Consolidation fee: If combining multiple orders, agents may charge ¥10-30 per additional package.

Step-by-Step: How to Buy from 1688

Here's the complete process for international buyers:

  1. Find your product on 1688.com — Use Chinese keywords or image search for best results. Compare multiple suppliers using the criteria above.
  2. Send the product link to your purchasing agent — Your agent will verify the supplier, check stock availability, and provide a quote including all costs.
  3. Confirm and pay — Pay your agent via PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer. The agent handles payment to the supplier using domestic Chinese payment methods.
  4. Agent receives and inspects — Products arrive at the agent's warehouse. They inspect quality, take photos, and send you a report for approval.
  5. Consolidation and shipping — If ordering from multiple suppliers, your agent consolidates everything into one package to save on shipping costs.
  6. Track your delivery — Receive tracking information and monitor your shipment until it arrives at your door.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many first-time 1688 buyers make these costly errors:

  • Choosing the cheapest supplier without verification — Low prices can indicate poor quality or scams. Always check supplier credentials.
  • Ignoring MOQ requirements — Some suppliers require minimum orders of 100+ units. Make sure the MOQ fits your needs.
  • Not accounting for shipping costs — A cheap product with expensive shipping may cost more overall. Calculate total landed cost.
  • Skipping quality inspection — Always have your agent inspect products before international shipping. Returns from overseas are extremely expensive.
  • Forgetting about customs regulations — Some products (electronics, cosmetics, food) have import restrictions. Check your country's regulations before ordering.

Why Use a Purchasing Agent for 1688?

While it's technically possible to buy from 1688 directly if you have Chinese payment methods and a Chinese address, using a purchasing agent offers significant advantages:

  • Payment handling — Agents pay suppliers using domestic Chinese payment methods, eliminating the need for you to set up Alipay China or a Chinese bank account.
  • Quality inspection — Professional inspection before shipping catches defects, wrong items, and quality issues.
  • Package consolidation — Combine orders from multiple suppliers into one shipment, saving 30-50% on international shipping.
  • Communication bridge — Agents negotiate with suppliers in Chinese, handling language barriers and cultural differences.
  • Shipping expertise — Agents know the best shipping methods for different products and destinations, optimizing cost and delivery time.

Pro Tip: When ordering from 1688, always ask your agent to request additional product photos from the supplier before purchasing. This helps verify the exact product you'll receive and reduces the risk of receiving items that don't match the listing.
