How to Pay on 1688.com: Foreign Buyer Guide

1688.com is China's largest wholesale marketplace, offering factory-direct prices that are often 30-50% cheaper than retail platforms like Taobao. However, for international buyers, paying on 1688 can be challenging. Here's everything you need to know.

Why Is Payment Difficult on 1688?

Unlike Taobao or AliExpress, 1688 is designed for domestic Chinese buyers. Most sellers only accept payments through Alipay linked to a Chinese bank account, or through bank transfers within China. International credit cards and PayPal are generally not supported.

Tips for Shopping on 1688

Check Minimum Order Quantities: Many 1688 sellers require minimum orders of 2-10 pieces. Always check the MOQ before ordering.

Compare Prices: The same product may be listed by multiple suppliers at different prices. Use image search to find the best deal.

Read Reviews: Look for sellers with high transaction volumes and positive feedback. A good seller rating is crucial for quality assurance.

Request Samples: For bulk orders, always request a sample first to verify quality before committing to a larger purchase.
